Organization

History, mission, oath, and structure

History

Founded by service-minded students at UAF, the group has grown into a campus platform for scouting, emergency readiness, community welfare, and leadership development.

Mission & Vision

To train disciplined youth leaders who serve Pakistan with courage, integrity, and practical skills while strengthening the university community.

Scout Oath

On my honour, I will do my best to serve Allah, Pakistan, and humanity; help others; and obey the scout law.

Scout Law

Trustworthy, loyal, helpful, friendly, courteous, kind, obedient, cheerful, thrifty, brave, clean, and reverent.

Message from the Group Leader

“Our duty is to convert youthful energy into service, discipline, and useful leadership for the university and the country.”

FAQ

Requirements and training process

Who can join?

Current UAF students with good conduct, availability for training, and willingness to serve.

Is prior scouting experience required?

No. Basic training covers discipline, patrol work, first aid, knots, camping, and service duty.

Are certificates available?

Certificates are issued for approved trainings, service drives, competitions, and major events.
